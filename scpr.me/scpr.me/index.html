What's coming up?
As the school year comes to a close, there are some exciting updates coming to School Progress. But don't worry — if you're returning to Hillside next year, you'll still be able to use it just like before.
Over the summer, I'll be working on a major update to School Progress that will expand the project to other schools in the city. This means students moving on to high school can continue using School Progress — now tailored to their new campus.
Starting next school year, I'll regularly update the schedules for each supported school with key events like holidays, minimum days, and other special schedules. These updates will be available for:
  • Hillside Middle School
  • Royal High School
  • Santa Susana High School
  • Simi Valley High School
If time allows, I plan to expand support to the remaining middle schools in Simi Valley as well. While I'll do my best to keep all schedules accurate and up to date, please understand that it may take time to gather and verify information for every school.
Thank you for continuing to use School Progress — and be ready for more improvements over the summer!
Along with these changes, I'll be working on improvements to School Progress. For those wondering, School Progress Shop is still a possibility, but it's not a priority right now. I want to make sure that the core experience of School Progress is as good as it can be before I start adding new features.
If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please feel free to reach out to me (search for Grant Garbe in Gmail)!
I hope you all have a great summer and school year next year!
Grant Garbe — Creator of School Progress
